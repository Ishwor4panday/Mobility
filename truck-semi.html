Key specifications

Range

Approx. 300–500 km depending on configuration and load.

Energy consumption

Approx. 1.5–2.0 kWh per km, depending on route and speed.

Powertrain

Multiple electric motors on the axles with torque vectoring for stability.

Fast charging

High‑power DC charging, capable of recovering a large part of the range in under an hour.

Gross combination weight

Designed for heavy freight operation with a high GCW rating.

Safety & driver assistance

Advanced driver assistance, stability control and braking systems.

Why this product matters

Use this section to explain the scientific and engineering ideas behind the truck and its impact on daily life.

Battery‑electric heavy trucks aim to reduce emissions from freight transport, which is a major contributor to global CO₂ output. They combine advances in battery technology, power electronics, aerodynamics, and vehicle control.

For students, this product connects topics like electric machines, thermal management, structural design, and traffic systems. Enthusiasts can use it as an example to compare different approaches to sustainable logistics.
